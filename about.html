Culture

Culture encompasses the shared beliefs, values, customs, traditions, behaviors, and artifacts that characterize a particular group of people. It's the way of life of a group of people, including their ideas, practices, customs, and social behavior that distinguishes them from other groups. Culture is not static; it evolves over time and is influenced by various factors such as history, geography, religion, technology, and interactions with other cultures.

Cultural Differences

Cultural differences refer to variations in beliefs, values, customs, behaviors, traditions, and other cultural aspects between different groups of people or societies. These differences can manifest in various ways, including language, communication styles, social norms, etiquette, religious practices, food preferences, and attitudes toward concepts such as time, family, and authority.
