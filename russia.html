Russian Culture

Russian culture is rich and diverse, shaped by its expansive history, geography, and a blend of Eastern European, Central Asian, and Slavic influences.

Russian Folk Music

Traditional songs and dances reflecting regional diversity, often accompanied by instruments like the balalaika and accordion.

Russian Icons

Religious paintings dating back to medieval times, known for their spiritual significance and artistic craftsmanship.

Maslenitsa

A week-long festival celebrating the end of winter with pancakes, music, and folk rituals.

Kremlin

Historic fortress complexes in Moscow and other cities, housing government buildings, museums, and cathedrals.
