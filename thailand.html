Thailand Culture

Thai culture is rich and diverse, blending ancient traditions with modern influences, and characterized by its vibrant festivals, cuisine, arts, and spiritual practices.

Khon

Classical masked dance-drama depicting scenes from the Ramakien (Thai version of the Ramayana), performed at royal courts and temples.

Wat Phra Kaew (Temple of the Emerald Buddha)

Located in Bangkok's Grand Palace complex, it houses Thailand's most sacred Buddhist relic.

Songkran

Thai New Year festival celebrated with water fights to symbolize washing away the past year's misfortunes and welcoming the new year with fresh hopes.

Muay Thai

Thai kickboxing known for its use of fists, elbows, knees, and shins in combat, and its rigorous training regimen.
