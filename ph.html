Philippine Culture

Filipino culture is marked by warmth, hospitality, resilience, and a strong sense of community.

Pagmamano

Pagmamano is a traditional Filipino gesture of respect, humility, and reverence. It is a gesture where a person takes the hand of an elder or someone of higher status, brings it to their forehead, and lightly touches the back of the hand to their forehead as a sign of respect.

Bayanihan

Bayanihan is a deeply ingrained Filipino cultural value that embodies the spirit of communal unity, cooperation, and helping one another.

Tinikling

A folk dance where dancers step gracefully between bamboo poles being clapped together rhythmically.

Simbang Gabi

A series of nine dawn masses before Christmas, combining Catholic devotion and Filipino hospitality.
